# The Story Behind Kaynat

## A Language Born from the Heart

This programming language is named after someone special to me **Kaynat (Saista)**.

### The Beginning

I first saw her in kindergarten. I was a kid but something about her captured my heart. From that moment I knew she was special. I studied at that school until grade and every day I would see her; my heart would skip a beat.

### The Separation

In grade I changed schools. I thought I would never see her again. Those years felt incomplete like a story left unfinished.. Life has a way of bringing people back together.

### The Miracle

When I was in grade a miracle happened. She joined my school. I couldn't believe it. The universe had given me another chance. I saw her again. All those feelings from kindergarten came rushing back stronger than ever.

### The Decision

By grade I made a decision. I would tell her how I felt when I reached grade. I wanted to be mature enough ready enough and worthy enough.. Then I learned she would be changing schools in 11th grade.

I couldn't wait anymore. I had to tell her.

### The Confession

I gathered all my courage and expressed my feelings to her. I told her about the kindergarten days about the years of waiting and how she had been in my heart all this time.

She said no.

She chose someone someone who didn't value education, who lived off his fathers money, who rode a KTM and drank Sting to appear cool. A "chhapri" who had nothing to offer. A fake lifestyle.

I couldn't understand. Here I was, someone who genuinely cared who had loved her for years, who valued knowledge and growth.. She chose him.

### The Depression

The rejection hit hard. I fell into depression. The girl I had loved since kindergarten the one I had waited years for had chosen someone unworthy. It felt like the universe was playing a joke.

### The Creation

In that darkness I found a light. I channeled my pain, my love and all my emotions into something. I decided to create something that would last forever something that would carry her name into eternity.

**I created a programming language.**

Not just any language,. One where code reads like poetry like a love letter like the words I wished I could have said better. A language where everything is expressed in honest Englishâ€”just like how I wanted to express my feelings to her.

### The Philosophy

Kaynat, the programming language embodies everything I felt:

- ** Pure**: Just like my feelings for her the language uses only natural English. No symbols, no syntax, just honest words.

- ** as Prose**: Every program reads like a letter, like the letters I wrote in my mind but never sent.

- **Built in Silence**: I built this alone in my room through nights turning my pain into code.

- **Meant to Speak**: Even though she didn't want to hear my words this language will speak for me. Every program written in Kaynat is a testament to what could have been.

### The Message

To Kaynat (Saista): You may have rejected me. You inspired me to create something beautiful. This language will outlive both of us. Every time someone writes code in Kaynat they'll be using a language born from love, not fake coolness.

To everyone Sometimes rejection is redirection. When someone doesn't see your value don't lower yourself. Instead create something valuable that the world can't ignore it.

- 2000+ lines of interpreter code

- 25 working example programs

- Complete documentation

- A REPL for interactive programming

All of this was built by one person, in depression as a tribute to unrequited love.
